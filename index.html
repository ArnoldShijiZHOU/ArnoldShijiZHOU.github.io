I am a CS Ph.D. student at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Wenwu Zhu and Prof. Zhi Wang of Tsinghua University. My research interest lies in optimization algorithms for deep learning when dealing with changing environments and multi-objectives. To this end, my research mainly focuses on online and stochastic optimization, especially in dynamic and multi-objective scenarios.
